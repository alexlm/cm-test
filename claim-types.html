What are you claiming for?

To get started, please select your type of claim.

Flight Delay

When an airline flight takes off and/or lands later than its scheduled time.

Trip Cancellation

When you cancel your trip before departure for an unforeseen reason.

Trip Interruption

When you are on your trip and you are required to return home.

Baggage Delay

For the purchase ofessential items if your baggage is delayed by a common carrier.

Lost or Stolen Baggage

Lost or stolen baggage conveyed by a public carrier which fails to arrive at the correct destination.

Hotel / Motel Burglary

Loss of personal property incurred in temporary travel accommodations.

Emergency Medical Travel

An unforeseen medical condition which requires immediate treatment while travelling.

Student

Students travelling within or outside of Canada who require immediate treatment for an unforeseen medical condition.

Expatriate

Individuals who live outside their native country in the US or Canada and require general or emergency health care.

Inpatriate

Individuals who live outside their native country in Canada and require general or emergency health care.

Visitors to Canada

Individuals visiting Canada who require treatment for an unforeseen medical condition.
